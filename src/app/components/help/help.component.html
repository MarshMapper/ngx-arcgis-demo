Using the Map

  • Click on dark green shaded "protected areas" or light green "unprotected areas" for details
  • In the popup for the selected area, click "Find Hotspots" (small bird icon) to find nearby eBird.org hotspots, or "Find Tide Stations" (small wave icon) to find nearby NOAA tide stations
  • Hotpots or tide stations found will appear as circles on map, click on circles for more details.
  • Use controls in the corners of the map to change the backround map, search for locations, or show / hide layers
