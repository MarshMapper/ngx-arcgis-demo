Using the Map

  • Click on dark green shaded "protected areas" or lighter "unprotected areas" for details
  • In the popup for the selected area, click on the action icons for local information:
    • "Find Hotspots" to find nearby eBird.org hotspots
    • "Find Tide Stations" to find nearby NOAA tide stations
    • "Weather Forecast" to get a short term weather forecast
  • Hotpots or tide stations found will appear as circles on map, click on circles for additional details.
  • Use controls in the corners of the map to change the backround map, search for locations, or show / hide layers
